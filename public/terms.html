AI concepts are reference art

Images from the AI Concept Studio are machine-generated references to help you communicate an idea. They are not final tattoo designs and are not guaranteed to be reproducible on skin. Your artist creates the actual custom design at your consult.

Appointments & deposits

Bookings are requests until confirmed by the studio. A deposit secures your date, applies to your final price, and is non-refundable on a no-show or same-day cancellation. Please give at least 48 hours' notice to reschedule.

Eligibility

You must be 18 or older with valid government photo ID to be tattooed. We may decline any request at our discretion, including designs we consider unsafe or unethical.

Content & conduct

Don't use the AI Studio to generate hateful, illegal, or infringing content. We may limit or block access that abuses the tool.
