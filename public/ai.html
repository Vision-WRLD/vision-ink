How to write your brief — the more you give, the sharper the concept

[subject] + [composition] + [style] + [placement] + [detail]

  1. Name the subject clearly, then add 2–3 descriptors — pose, objects, symmetry.
  2. Set the vibe: “negative space”, “fine detail”, “bold solid fills”, or “minimal”.
  3. Add placement: forearm, sternum, full sleeve — it shapes the composition.
  4. Keep it a phrase, roughly 8–20 words. Pick a Style above; generate again for fresh takes.
