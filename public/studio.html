Process

Idea to healed skin

Four steps, no surprises.

Concept

Bring an idea — or one from the AI Studio. Free 30-minute consult to lock subject, size and placement.

Custom draw

Your artist draws a bespoke design and shares a preview. We adjust until it's exactly right.

The session

Single-use needles, fresh ink caps, sterile field. We work at your pace with breaks whenever you need.

Aftercare

You leave with a full kit, written instructions, and a lifetime touch-up promise on our line work.

Aftercare

Heal it right

  • 1Leave the wrap on for the first 2–4 hours, then wash gently with fragrance-free soap.
  • 2Pat dry, apply a thin layer of the balm in your kit. Twice daily for two weeks.
  • 3No pools, saunas, or direct sun while healing. Don't pick or scratch.
  • 4Something feels off? Message us — free touch-ups on our line work, always.
