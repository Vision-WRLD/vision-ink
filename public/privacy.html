What we collect

When you request a consult we collect the name, email, and details you enter in the booking form. That's it — we don't run trackers, ad pixels, or third-party analytics on this site.

AI Studio prompts

Text you type into the AI Concept Studio is sent to our image provider only to generate your three concepts, then discarded. We do not save your prompts or the generated images on our servers, and we don't tie them to your identity.

How we use it

Booking details are used solely to reply to you and arrange your appointment. We never sell or share your information with anyone outside the studio.

Your choices

Ask us to delete your booking details at any time by emailing the studio. We'll remove them from our records within 30 days.
